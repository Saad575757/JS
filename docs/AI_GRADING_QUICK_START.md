## Step-by-Step Setup

### 1. Enable AI Grading for an Assignment

1. Navigate to your class
2. Go to the **Assignments** tab
3. Find the assignment you want to enable AI grading for
4. Click the **robot icon (ü§ñ)** next to the assignment

### 2. Configure Settings

In the AI Grading Settings modal:

#### Enable AI Grading
- Toggle **"Enable AI Grading"** to ON

#### Choose Grading Mode

**Option A: Manual Review** (Recommended for first-time use)
- AI analyzes submissions and suggests grades
- You review and approve each grade before students see them
- Perfect for getting comfortable with AI grading

**Option B: Automatic**
- AI grades immediately upon submission
- Students get instant feedback
- Best for objective assignments with clear criteria

#### Add Custom Instructions (Optional)
Help the AI understand your grading priorities:
```
Examples:
- "Focus on code efficiency and readability"
- "Penalize late submissions by 10 points"
- "Give partial credit for showing work"
- "Check for proper citations in MLA format"
```

#### Generate Rubric (Optional)
- Click **"Generate Rubric"** to let AI create grading criteria
- Review and adjust the suggested criteria
- Share with students for transparency

### 3. Save Your Settings
Click **"Save Settings"** to activate AI grading!

## Using AI Grading

### Manual Review Mode

#### Step 1: Students Submit
Students complete and submit their assignments as usual.

#### Step 2: AI Analyzes
AI automatically analyzes each submission and generates:
- Proposed grade (0-100)
- Detailed feedback
- Strengths identified
- Areas for improvement
- Criteria breakdown

#### Step 3: Review Pending Grades
1. Click **"Pending AI Grades"** button in the Assignments tab
2. Review each grade:
   - See proposed grade and feedback
   - Read AI analysis (strengths/improvements)
   - Check criteria breakdown
3. For each grade:
   - **Approve**: Grade is applied, student sees it
   - **Reject**: Provide reason, grade manually if needed

#### Step 4: Students Receive Feedback
Once approved, students see:
- Their grade
- Detailed feedback
- Specific strengths and areas to improve

### Automatic Mode

1. Students submit assignments
2. AI grades instantly
3. Students immediately see:
   - Grade
   - Detailed feedback
   - Analysis
4. No manual review needed!

## Alternative: Grade Individual Submissions

For manual mode, you can also trigger AI grading for specific students:

1. Go to **Assignments** tab
2. Click **"View Submissions"** on an assignment
3. Navigate to a specific student's submission
4. Click **"AI Grade"** button
5. AI analyzes that submission
6. Review and approve in "Pending AI Grades"

## Common Questions

### Q: Can I edit AI-suggested grades?
**A:** Yes! In manual mode, you can reject AI grades and manually grade instead. You can also modify the grade before approving.

### Q: What if AI gets it wrong?
**A:** Simply reject the grade and provide your own. You can also add feedback about why it was wrong to improve future grading.

### Q: Can students see that AI graded their work?
**A:** Students see the grade and feedback. Whether you disclose AI usage is up to your institution's policy.

### Q: What types of assignments work best?
**A:** AI grading works well for:
- Essays and written assignments
- Code submissions
- Problem sets with clear solutions
- Research papers
- Any assignment with objective criteria

### Q: Can I use AI grading for discussions?
**A:** Yes, but manual review mode is recommended for subjective content like discussions and reflections.

### Q: How does AI handle plagiarism?
**A:** AI focuses on grading quality. For plagiarism detection, use dedicated plagiarism tools alongside AI grading.

## Troubleshooting

### Problem: AI grades seem inconsistent
**Solution:** 
- Add more detailed custom instructions
- Generate and use a rubric
- Review assignment description for clarity

### Problem: No pending grades showing up
**Solution:**
- Ensure AI grading is enabled for the assignment
- Check that students have actually submitted
- Verify you're in manual mode (auto mode has no pending grades)

### Problem: Can't enable AI grading
**Solution:**
- Verify you're logged in as a teacher
- Check that the assignment hasn't been deleted
- Contact support if issue persists
