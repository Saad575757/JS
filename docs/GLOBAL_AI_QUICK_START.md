## FAQs

### Q: Can I still manually grade if AI is enabled?
**A:** Yes! In Manual Review mode, you can reject AI grades and manually grade instead.

### Q: What if I want different settings for different assignments?
**A:** Global settings provide consistency, but you can still manually grade specific assignments if needed.

### Q: Can I disable AI for just one assignment?
**A:** Currently, AI applies globally. You can use Manual Review mode and reject specific assignment grades.

### Q: What happens to existing grades?
**A:** Existing grades remain unchanged. AI only grades new submissions.

### Q: Can students see that AI graded them?
**A:** Students see grades and feedback. Disclosing AI usage is per your institution's policy.

### Q: How accurate is AI grading?
**A:** Start with Manual Review to verify. Most teachers find 85-95% accuracy after tuning instructions.
